Our Story

About Us

Heywood, Greater Manchester

Proud to Serve
Our Community

Heywood Chippy sits right at the heart of the local community. Based on Wham Street, we've built our name on one simple promise — proper food, made properly.

Everything we serve is prepared fresh. Our fish is battered to order, our chips are cooked to perfection, and every meal that leaves our counter is something we're genuinely proud to put our name to.

We're not a chain. We're not a franchise. We're your local chippy — and that means something to us. When you walk through our door or pick up the phone, you're supporting a local Heywood business that genuinely cares about what it serves and who it serves it to.

From classic fish suppers to kebabs, burgers, and our incredible Family Special, there's something for everyone. Come see us — or give us a ring.
