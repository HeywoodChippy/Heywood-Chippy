Who We Are

More Than a Takeaway

Heywood Chippy has been a fixture on Wham Street for as long as most people can remember. We're not a chain and we have no plans to become one. We're a local takeaway run by local people who care deeply about what they put out the pass. Every piece of fish we serve is sourced fresh and battered when you order it. Not before. Not hours earlier. When you order it.

The menu has grown better and stronger: kebabs, wraps, burgers, salt and pepper. But the heart of what we do has never changed. Proper fish and chips. Cooked right. Priced fairly. The kind of chippy night your family will actually remember.

We know most of our regulars by name. We know who has their curry sauce on the side and who always asks for extra salt. That's not a marketing line, that's just what happens when you stay in one place, do one thing properly, and genuinely care about the people you feed. Come in, see for yourself.

What Drives Us

The Way We Do Things

Uncompromising Freshness

Every fish that comes through our door is sourced fresh that day. We don't buy in bulk, we don't freeze, and we don't cut corners. Our batter is made fresh, our oil is clean, and everything is cooked to order, not left sitting under a heat lamp. If it's not right, it doesn't go out the window.

Genuinely Local

We live in this county. We shop at the same places as you. Being genuinely local means your money stays in Heywood, our team are Heywood people, and when you need something sorted we're actually here not a faceless head office somewhere else in the country.

Made to Order, Every Time

When you call in or phone through your order, the fryer goes on. That's the deal. It means you might wait a few minutes longer than a chain would make you wait, but what you get in return is food that's actually fresh, actually hot, and actually worth eating. We think that's a fair trade.

Honest Through and Through

Real portions at fair prices. No sneaky small sizes disguised as regular. No watered-down curry sauce to cut costs. No gimmicks, no loyalty schemes, no time-limited offers designed to make you feel like you're missing out. Just good food at a price that doesn't make your eyes water, because that's how it should be.
